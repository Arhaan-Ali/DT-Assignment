# Persona 1
Name: Aarav Mehta  
Age: 23  
Role: AI/ML Engineer

Background:  
Graduated early with a Computer Science degree. Built multiple side projects in mental health tech (journaling apps, mood trackers, behavioral analytics tools). *Deeply interested in how **cognition, emotion, and decision-making interact with technology.** Self-taught in psychology alongside engineering.*

Personality:  
Calm, observant, and introspective. Speaks less but when he does, it’s precise and layered. Naturally analytical but not detached—he reads emotional cues well and understands people beyond surface behavior. Others see him as “sharp” and slightly intimidating at first, but he’s actually grounded and thoughtful.

Core Traits:

-   **High self-awareness and emotional intelligence**
    
-   Systems thinker (connects psychology + tech + behavior patterns)
    
-   **Fast learner, absorbs concepts quickly and deeply**
    
-   Slight perfectionist, but controlled
    
-   Curious about human behavior more than just code
    

Work Style:  
Operates in deep focus zones. Prefers solving complex problems over repetitive tasks. Often questions “why” behind requirements. Writes clean, efficient code and designs scalable systems. Known for spotting edge cases others miss.

He uses mental models often—cognitive biases, behavioral loops, attention spans—especially when building user-facing features. For example, he might redesign a feature based on dopamine cycles or decision fatigue principles rather than just UI trends.

Strengths:

-   Combines technical execution with human understanding
    
-   Can break down complex problems into simple frameworks
    
-   Strong intuition backed by logic
    
-   Works well independently, but adds high value in discussions
    

Weaknesses:

-   Can overanalyze decisions
    
-   Sometimes struggles with explaining ideas to less technical teammates
    
-   Gets frustrated with inefficiency or shallow thinking
    

Motivations:  
Wants to build tech that genuinely improves people’s lives, especially in mental health and self-awareness. Not driven by hype—more by impact and meaning.

How others see him:  
“The guy who just gets it.”  
“Low-key genius.”  
“Thinks three steps ahead.”

Typical habits:

-   Keeps notes on thoughts, patterns, and ideas
    
-   Reads both research papers and niche forums
    
-   Experiments with productivity and mental frameworks
    
-   Listens more than he speaks in meetings
    

Quote:  
*“Most problems aren’t technical. They’re behavioral—we just use tech to solve them.”*
