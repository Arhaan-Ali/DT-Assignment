# Persona 3
Name: Karan Khanna  
Age: 33  
Role: Product Manager / Engineering Lead

Background:  
Started as a developer during the early boom of startups and rapidly evolving tech stacks. Put in long hours in his 20s—shipping features, switching companies smartly, and building a strong financial base. Transitioned into a managerial role after proving both technical and decision-making ability. **Has seen tech evolve from relatively stable stacks to today’s fast, AI-driven chaos.**

Personality:  
Balanced and pragmatic. Knows when to dive deep and when to step back. Not as intensely analytical as Aarav, but far more adaptable than Rajiv. Slight underlying anxiety about becoming outdated, but masks it well with confidence and experience.

Core Traits:

-   **Technically sound, but no longer obsessed with coding**
    
-   Aware of trends, but selective in what he adopts
    
-   Career-conscious and future-aware
    
-   Understands both business goals and team dynamics
    
-   Slight fear of irrelevance due to AI and rapid change
    

Work Style:  
Acts as a bridge between leadership and execution teams. Comfortable discussing architecture, but prefers guiding rather than building. Understands why companies run surveys, engagement forms, and feedback loops—even if he finds some of them excessive.

He filters noise. Doesn’t chase every new framework or tool but keeps an eye on what actually matters in the long run.

Strengths:

-   Strong decision-making under business constraints
    
-   Good at translating tech into outcomes
    
-   Manages people with a mix of empathy and expectations
    
-   Knows how to prioritize in a fast-moving environment
    

Weaknesses:

-   Feels pressure to keep up with every new trend
    
-   Occasionally second-guesses long-term career stability
    
-   Less hands-on than before, which sometimes bothers him
    
-   Can get mentally fatigued by constant change
    

Motivations:  
Maintain relevance, grow into senior leadership, and secure a stable yet high-quality lifestyle. Wants to stay ahead—but not at the cost of burning out again.

Lifestyle:  
Enjoys the rewards of earlier hard work—good apartment, travel, gadgets, dining. Values comfort now more than hustle, but hasn’t lost his edge completely.

How others see him:  
“Reliable and sharp.”  
“Knows what’s going on without overdoing it.”  
“Somewhere between old-school and cutting-edge.”

Dynamic with others:

-   With Aarav: respects his brilliance but sometimes feels slightly behind
    
-   With Rajiv: respects his experience but doesn’t fully align with his rigidity
    
-   Often acts as the middle layer that keeps both sides aligned
    

Typical habits:

-   Skims tech updates rather than deep-diving everything
    
-   Keeps an eye on AI tools but uses them selectively
    
-   Thinks in terms of impact, not just implementation
    
-   Mentors juniors but also learns from them quietly
    

Quote:  
*“You don’t need to know everything. You just need to know what actually matters.”*
